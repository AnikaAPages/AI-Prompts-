01 Content
Caption Batch Generator
Write 10 engaging social media captions for a [brand/niche] brand targeting [audience] on [platform: IG / TikTok / Facebook / LinkedIn / X]. Match tone and length to the platform's culture. Mix educational, inspirational, and promotional angles. Include 1–2 CTAs per caption.
02 Content
Thought Leadership Post
Write a thought leadership post for [platform: IG / TikTok / Facebook / LinkedIn / X] where I share a counterintuitive insight about [industry topic]. Open with a bold hook, build with a short personal story or data point, and close with a question to drive comments. Match length and format to the platform.
03 Content
Carousel / Slide Series Script
Create a 7-slide carousel or slide series script on "[topic]" for [platform: IG / TikTok / Facebook / LinkedIn / X]. Slide 1: bold hook. Slides 2–6: one key point each with a short explanation. Slide 7: summary + CTA. Keep each slide to 1–2 punchy sentences.
04 Content
Serial / Thread Post Writer
Write a 10-part thread or serial post about [topic] for [platform: IG / TikTok / Facebook / LinkedIn / X] targeting [audience]. Part 1 must hook with a bold claim or surprising stat. Format each part to fit the platform's character or format limits. End with a post that drives shares or saves.
05 Content
Short-Form Video Script
Write a 30-second video script for [brand/creator] about [topic] for [platform: IG Reels / TikTok / Facebook / LinkedIn / X]. Include a hook in the first 3 seconds, clear value delivery in the middle, and a scroll-stopping CTA at the end. Add b-roll or on-screen text suggestions in brackets.
06 Content
Repurpose Blog → Social Posts
Repurpose the following blog post into 5 distinct social media posts — one for each of these platforms: Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, LinkedIn, and X. Adapt the tone, format, and length to fit each platform's culture and audience expectations. Blog post: [paste blog content]
07 Content
Holiday / Seasonal Content
Create 5 social media posts for [brand] around [holiday/season]. Make them feel authentic and on-brand — not generic. Include a mix of formats: one story, one caption, one poll or question, and two short-form copy options.
08 Content
Behind-the-Scenes Series
Write a 5-part behind-the-scenes content series for [brand] showing [process/event] on [platform: IG / TikTok / Facebook / LinkedIn / X]. Each post should feel candid and human. Include suggested interactive prompts — polls, questions, or reaction hooks — suited to the platform's engagement features.
09 Content
Customer Testimonial Rewrite
Rewrite the following raw customer review into a polished, platform-ready social post for [platform: IG / TikTok / Facebook / LinkedIn / X] for [brand]. Keep the customer's authentic voice but make it scannable, emotionally resonant, and on-brand. Add a brief setup line and a CTA. Review: [paste review]
10 Content
Event Coverage Content Plan
Create a real-time content plan for [brand] covering [event: bridal expo / trunk show / brand activation / local festival] across [platforms]. Include: pre-event teaser posts, 3–5 during-event content moments with copy and format suggestions, and 2 post-event recap formats. Flag which moments work best for Stories vs. feed vs. video.
11 Content
Cross-Platform Repurpose Workflow
Take this top-performing social post: [paste post + platform it ran on]. Repurpose it into 4 new formats for the remaining platforms — Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, LinkedIn, and X — keeping the core idea but rewriting the hook, format, and length to feel native to each. Flag any format that doesn't suit the content and explain why.

Platform-Specific Strategy Brief
Create a 90-day social media strategy for [brand] focusing on [platform]. Include: growth goals, content themes, posting frequency, engagement tactics, and 3 KPIs to track. Format as a brief I can share with a client or team.
18 Strategy
Campaign Launch Plan
Write a social media launch plan for [product/event/campaign]. Include a pre-launch teaser phase (1 week out), launch day content, and a post-launch engagement push (3 days after). Specify content types, copy directions, and timing for each phase.

26 Strategy
Influencer & Creator Collab Brief
Write a creator collaboration brief for [brand] targeting [creator type: micro-influencer / local lifestyle creator / bridal content creator]. Include: brand overview, campaign goal, content deliverables, key messages to hit, what NOT to say, approval process, and compensation structure. Tone should feel like a partnership, not a directive.
27 Strategy
Seasonal Campaign Calendar
Build a seasonal social media campaign calendar for [brand: bridal / regional lifestyle] covering [season or date range]. Factor in industry-specific peaks — for bridal: engagement season (Nov–Feb), trunk shows, and wedding season (May–Oct); for lifestyle: local events, editorial cycles, and regional milestones. Output as a table with Month, Campaign Theme, Key Content Moments, and Platform Focus.

Based on current platform behavior, identify: 5 content trends gaining traction in this space, 3 audience conversation themes worth joining, and 2 formats or styles that are overused and losing engagement. For each trend, suggest a specific, on-brand post idea for [brand].
